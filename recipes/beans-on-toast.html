Beans on Toast

Description

Beans on toast has been eaten for years every day in the UK for breakfast, lunch and dinner. Its a super simple dish, but its comfort food on a plate for many of us.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Beans
  • Cheese
  • Butter
  • Sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. Put Bread in toaster
  2. Put Beans on the hob
  3. Butter toast once done in toaster
  4. Pour beans onto the toast once warmed through
  5. Enjoy
