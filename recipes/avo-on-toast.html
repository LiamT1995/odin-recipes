Avocade on Toast

Description

Make this simple speedy breakfast with just a few ingredients. Our smashed avocado on toast uses crusty sourdough bread and a pinch of chilli for a kick.

Ingredients

  • Bread Sour Dough
  • Avocado
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Chilli Powder optional
  • Lemon/Lime juice optional

Steps

  1. Toast Sourdough bread
  2. Scoop out the center of avocado and remove the giant pip
  3. Squeeze lemon/lime juice ontop
  4. Take a pinch of Chilli powder and sprinkle ontop of avocado
  5. Do the same as above for the Salt & Pepper
  6. Enjoy!
